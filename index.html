Hand-crafted, modern, thoughtfully designed furniture

See more of John Gallagher's work

Piece Furniture offers a fine balance of aesthetics, quality workmanship and functional ability. Design, attention to detail, functional requisites, and fine timber selection are all of the utmost importance.

Piece Furniture was formed in 2003 by John Gallagher to provide hand crafted furniture on a commission basis in addition to limited production works.

John Gallagher is a craftsman and teacher with practical skills and an eye for strong aesthetics. He is part of a new movement of contemporary fine furniture makers and collectors respecting our valuable timber resources. His vision is to move away from our throw-away-world by creating furniture that will extend and be cherished beyond our life time.
