John Gallagher

A craftsman and teacher with practical skills and an eye for strong aesthetics. He is part of a new movement of contemporary fine furniture makers and collectors respecting our valuable timber resources. His vision is to move away from our throw-away-world by creating furniture that will extend and be cherished beyond our life time.
